Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What is ApnaHood?
ApnaHood is a web application that helps students and working professionals find PGs and rented houses. We also provide delivery of furniture to PGs or rented houses and accept online payments.
How do I search for a PG or rented house on ApnaHood?
You can search for PGs or rented houses by entering your desired location and other preferences in the search bar on our homepage. The app will display a list of available options that match your criteria.
Can I filter my search results?
Yes, you can filter your search results based on various criteria such as price, location, amenities, and more.
Do you offer furniture delivery to PGs or rented houses?
Yes, ApnaHood provides delivery of furniture to PGs or rented houses. You can select the furniture you need from our catalog, and we will deliver it to your location.
How do I make a payment on ApnaHood?
You can make payments online through our secure payment gateway. We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards, net banking, and popular digital wallets.
Is my payment information secure?
Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and security measures to ensure that your payment information is safe and secure.
Can I cancel my booking?
Yes, you can cancel your booking. Please refer to our cancellation policy for detailed information on the process and any applicable fees.
How do I contact customer support?
You can contact our customer support team via email at support@apnahood.com or call us at our customer service number. We are available 24/7 to assist you with any queries or issues.
